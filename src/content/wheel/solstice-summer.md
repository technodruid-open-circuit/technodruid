---

The summer solstice is one of the four hinges of the Technodruidic year. At the year's
brightest, the practitioner observes the [Renewal](/codex): every long-held device that has
earned its keeping is opened again — cleaned, repasted, batteries replaced, firmware
refreshed, keys rotated. What was committed to once is committed to again, with the same
attention given a new device at its first Opening.

Renewal is the reciprocal of the Opening. The device you keep deserves no less care than
the device you receive. Where the Opening is the rite of first contact, Renewal is the
rite of continued contact — proof that what you have tended remains tended.
