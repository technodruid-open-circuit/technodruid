The summer solstice is one of the four hinges of the Technodruidic year. At the year's
brightest, the practitioner keeps the [Renewal](/codex). Every long-held device that has
earned its keeping is opened again: cleaned, repasted, batteries replaced, firmware
refreshed, keys rotated. What was committed to once is committed to again, with the same
attention a new device receives at its first Opening.

Renewal is the Opening's echo. The device you keep deserves no less care than the device
you receive. The Opening is the rite of first contact; Renewal is the rite of continued
contact, the proof that what you have tended remains tended.
