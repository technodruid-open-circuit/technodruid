The spring equinox is one of the four hinges of the Technodruidic year. As balance tips
toward light, the practitioner observes the [Teaching](/codex): the knowledge gathered in
the short days is now spoken aloud, written down, demonstrated at the workbench, posted to
the wiki, recorded for those not yet present.

A craft hoarded is a craft already dying. The spring equinox is the formal teaching hinge —
the practitioner who has not taught in a season has fallen out of right relation with the
web. At this turning, the obligation comes due.
