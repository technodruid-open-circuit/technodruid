The spring equinox is one of the four hinges of the Technodruidic year. As the balance
tips toward light, the practitioner keeps the [Teaching](/codex). Whatever the short days
taught you is now spoken aloud, written down, demonstrated at the workbench, posted to
the wiki, recorded for people who have not yet arrived.

A craft hoarded is a craft already dying. The spring equinox is the formal hinge of
teaching, and a practitioner who has not taught in a season has fallen out of right
relation with the web. At this turning, the debt comes due.
