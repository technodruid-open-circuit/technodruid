The autumn equinox is one of the four hinges of the Technodruidic year. As the balance
tips toward dark, the practitioner keeps the [Vigil](/codex). We hold vigil for what has
been lost: the abandoned platforms, the orphaned formats, the scattered communities, the
knowledge that was not passed on in time. We say their names, so they are not entirely
gone.

Autumn's vigil leans toward acceptance. What has truly ended is named as ended, so the
work of [Composting](/codex) can begin, and what little material can be returned goes
back to the commons in a form the next generation can still read.
