The autumn equinox is one of the four hinges of the Technodruidic year. As balance tips
toward dark, the practitioner observes the [Vigil](/codex): we hold vigil for what has
been lost — abandoned platforms, orphaned formats, dispersed communities, knowledge that
was not passed on in time. We name them, so they are not entirely gone.

Autumn's vigil leans toward acceptance. What has reached its end is named as ended so the
practice of [Composting](/codex) can begin, and the material — what little can be returned
— is sent back to the commons in a form the next generation can still read.
