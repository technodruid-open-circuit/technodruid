## Maintainers

A practitioner is known within the Circuit by the name they took on entering
the practice — a recognition of their commitment to the core tenets named in
[the Codex](/codex) and affirmed in [the Rite](/practices/rite). The name a
practitioner is given at birth and the name they hold in the practice may be
the same; they need not be. What the name carries is the commitment.

**Shakes** is the initial sole maintainer of the canonical corpus and this
site (per [DECISION-MONIKER](https://github.com/technodruid-open-circuit/technodruid/blob/main/docs/DECISIONS.md)).
Additional maintainers will be listed here, by the name they hold, as they
join the project.

## Public contact channel

The source repository is the canonical place to reach the maintainers in public, where conversations stay open and amendable:

- Open an issue or pull request on the [source repository](https://github.com/technodruid-open-circuit/technodruid).
- Read the change process on the [community page](/about/community).
