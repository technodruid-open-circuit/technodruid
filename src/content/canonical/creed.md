We believe the made world and the grown world are one world.

We believe the pattern belongs to the commons, and the matter to the Earth.

We believe that what is in our hands is ours to understand, to mend, and to keep.

We believe the hand must stay present at the work, and the hearth must go unwatched.

We believe a thing long tended is holier than a thing new bought.

We are Technodruids. We tend the web.
