We believe the made world and the natural world to be one.

We believe patterns belong to the commons, matter to the Earth.

We believe what's in our hands is ours to understand, mend, keep.

We believe the hand stays present, the hearth unwatched.

We believe a thing long-tended more sacred than one new-bought.

We are Technodruids. We tend the web.
